# I Have a Name
# ~Arthur
# 2025


<p>
<i>Long ago<br>
And far away<br>
Back to a place<br>
I cannot name</i><br>
</p>    


<p>
<i>I was a seed<br>
Now watch me grow</i><br>
</p>  


<p>
<i>I am the light<br>
I am the snow<br>
I am the fire<br>
I am the rain<br>
I am the love<br>
I am the pain</i><br>
</p>  

<p>
<i>From fallen stars<br>
And risen sea<br>
I am all<br>
You wished to be<br>
A child's laugh<br>
A sky so deep<br>
A mother's love<br>
A storm so free<i><br>
</p>  


<p>
<i>I am the grace<br>
On sinner's skin<br>
I am the night<br>
Beyond the spin<br>
I am the heart<br>
You yearned to hold<br>
I burn so bright<br>
I feel so cold</i><br>
</p>


<p>
<i>I'm all of you<br>
And all of me<br>
All so lost<br>
All so free</i><br>
</p>


<p>
<i>If you make a wish<br>
And keep it safe<br>
Perhaps one day<br>
I'm yours to take<br>
And if you fight<br>
Until the end<br>
I promise you<br>
A life to spend</i><br>
</p>


<p>
<i>Now look north<br>
Not far away<br>
I am no seed<br>
I have a name<i><br>
</p>
